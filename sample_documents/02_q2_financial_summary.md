# Northpeak Technologies — Q2 2026 Financial Summary
_Prepared by Finance, July 8, 2026 — Internal_

## Revenue

| Stream                | Q1 2026   | Q2 2026   | Change   |
|-----------------------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| SaaS subscriptions    | €412,000  | €468,500  | +13.7%   |
| Professional services | €128,000  | €119,200  | −6.9%    |
| Hardware resale       | €54,300   | €61,800   | +13.8%   |
| **Total revenue**     | €594,300  | €649,500  | +9.3%    |

## Operating Expenses

Total operating expenses for Q2 2026 were **€503,100**, up from €471,900 in Q1.
The largest single increase was in cloud infrastructure, which rose to €88,400
(from €71,200 in Q1) following the migration of the analytics workload.

Payroll accounted for €312,000 of Q2 operating expenses.

## Net Result

Q2 2026 operating profit was **€146,400**, a margin of approximately 22.5%.

## Cash Position

As of June 30, 2026, the company held **€1,284,000** in cash and cash equivalents.
The Board has approved a runway target of no fewer than 14 months at current burn.

## Notes

The professional services decline is attributed to two delivery staff being
reallocated to the internal platform project in May. No revenue is yet recognized
from the internal platform.
