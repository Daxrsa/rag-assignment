# Master Services Agreement — Northpeak Technologies & Acme Cloud Services
_Effective March 1, 2026_

## 2. Term and Renewal

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of
**twelve (12) months**. It renews automatically for successive twelve-month terms
unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **60 days**
before the end of the then-current term.

## 3. Fees and Payment

Customer shall pay the Service Fees within **30 days** of the invoice date (Net 30).
Late payments accrue interest at **1.5% per month**. The monthly Service Fee for the
Standard Tier is **€6,200**, billed monthly in arrears.

Fees may be increased by Provider no more than once per twelve-month period, and any
increase is capped at **5%** of the then-current fee.

## 4. Service Levels

Provider guarantees **99.9% monthly uptime**. If uptime falls below this threshold in
any calendar month, Customer is entitled to a service credit equal to 10% of that
month's Service Fee. Service credits are the sole and exclusive remedy for downtime.

## 5. Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach if the breach remains
uncured **30 days** after written notice. Upon termination, Provider will make
Customer data available for export for a period of **30 days**, after which it may be
permanently deleted.

## 6. Liability

Provider's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees
paid by Customer in the **twelve months** preceding the claim.
