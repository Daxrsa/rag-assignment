# Northpeak Technologies — Employee Handbook (Excerpt)
_Version 3.2 — Effective January 1, 2026_

## 4. Paid Time Off (PTO)

Full-time employees accrue **1.75 days of PTO per month**, for a total of 21 days
per calendar year. PTO begins accruing on the employee's first day but may not be
used during the first 90 days of employment.

Unused PTO may be carried over to the following year up to a maximum of **10 days**.
Any balance above 10 days on December 31 is forfeited and is not paid out.

PTO requests of 3 or more consecutive days must be submitted at least 14 days in
advance and approved by the employee's direct manager.

## 5. Remote Work

Northpeak operates on a hybrid model. Employees are expected to be in the office a
**minimum of two days per week**, with Tuesday designated as a company-wide
in-office "anchor day." Fully remote arrangements require VP-level approval and are
reviewed every six months.

## 6. Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses are reimbursed when submitted within **30 days** of the expense
date, accompanied by an itemized receipt. Meals during business travel are
reimbursed up to **€45 per day**. Expenses submitted after 60 days will not be
reimbursed except in documented exceptional circumstances approved by Finance.

Personal vehicle mileage for business travel is reimbursed at **€0.36 per kilometer**.

## 7. Probation

New employees serve a probationary period of **6 months**. During this period either
party may terminate the employment relationship with two weeks' notice.
